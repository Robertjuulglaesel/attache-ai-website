The problem with cloud AI

Every time you use ChatGPT,

Not maliciously. Just structurally. Your conversations train their models. Sit behind their security. Get stored on their servers — indefinitely.

For casual questions, that's fine. For sensitive business strategy, client details, personal decisions, financial planning — that's a problem you're probably not thinking about until it's too late.
