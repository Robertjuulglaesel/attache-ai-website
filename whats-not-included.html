Anthropic Claude · OpenAI GPT-4o · Google Gemini

The brain of your assistant. You pay the AI provider directly based on how much you use it — charged per message, not per month. Light users pay less. Heavy users pay more. We help you choose the right model for your budget and use case.

Most clients land between $20–50/mo. Power users who rely on the assistant all day may reach $80–100/mo.

Voice Calling

Vapi.ai + Twilio

Two services work together to give your assistant a real phone number and voice. Vapi handles the AI voice layer; Twilio provides the actual phone number and call routing. Both are billed by the minute — you only pay when calls happen.

Casual users (a few calls/week): ~$10–15/mo. Active users (daily calls): ~$25–40/mo.
