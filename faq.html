The basics

What is Attaché AI?

What is Attaché AI?

A done-for-you service that builds you a personal AI assistant on your own hardware. We handle all the technical setup. You get an assistant that knows your life, runs privately, and gets more valuable every day.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT resets every session and runs on OpenAI's servers. Your Attaché assistant has permanent memory, runs on your own hardware, handles calls and Telegram, and compounds in value daily. ChatGPT is a tool you use. Attaché AI is an assistant that knows you. See the full comparison →

What can my assistant do?

Manage email. Handle your calendar. Text you on Telegram. Make and receive phone calls on your behalf. Research anything. Set reminders. Automate recurring tasks. Book appointments. Learn your preferences. And keep getting better over time.

Who is Attaché AI for?

Professionals, executives, and entrepreneurs who want the leverage of a personal assistant — without the full-time hire, without months of DIY setup, and without giving up ownership of their data. Read more about executive use cases →

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe?

Is my data private?

Yes. Your assistant runs on your hardware or a dedicated server we set up exclusively for you. We have no backend access to your conversations. Neither does anyone else. Learn more about privacy →

Does Attaché AI train on my conversations?

No. Your conversations stay on your machine. We have no access to them and they are never used to train any model.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

It stays on your hardware. You keep everything — your assistant, your context, your history. We don't have it, so we can't take it.

Hardware & Setup

What do I need?

Do I need to own a Mac?

No. If you have a Mac, we use it. If you don't, we provision a dedicated cloud Mac Mini for you — your own machine, not a shared server. It's included in the We Handle Everything plan.

How long does setup take?

3–5 business days after your 30-minute onboarding call. See the full process →

What is OpenClaw?

The open-source AI agent framework your assistant runs on. It's what gives your assistant memory, real-world tools, and the ability to take action — not just answer questions.

Can my assistant make phone calls?

Yes. Inbound and outbound. It handles calls the way a human assistant would — takes the call, handles it, and reports back.

What messaging apps does it work with?

Telegram out of the box. iMessage and WhatsApp can be connected during setup.

What if I need something new?

We build it. New capability, new automation, new integration — we add it. Your assistant grows as your needs evolve.
